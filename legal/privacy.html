Privacy Policy

We collect the minimum needed to run the service. We never sell your data.

2. What we do not collect

  • Card numbers, CVV, or full bank details (handled entirely by Stripe).
  • Brokerage credentials, holdings, or trade history. We never connect to your broker.
  • Social Security Number, driver's license, or government ID.
  • Marketing trackers from third parties — we do not use Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, or similar.

3. Cookies

We use a small number of strictly-necessary cookies:

  • dash_jwt — your session, HttpOnly + Secure + SameSite=Lax, 7-day expiry.
  • session — legacy session (will be removed in a future release).

We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

4. Where data lives

Application data is stored on Microsoft Azure in the US. Stripe stores payment data per its own policy. Email delivery uses a transactional provider (Azure Communication Services / SendGrid).

5. How we use it

  • To run the service and your account.
  • To bill you and prevent fraud.
  • To improve the product (aggregate analytics — not individual profiling).
  • To comply with legal obligations (tax, subpoena, etc.).

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

6. Sharing

Limited sharing only with sub-processors strictly necessary to run the service:

  • Microsoft Azure — hosting and storage.
  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Email provider — transactional email delivery.

7. Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active and for up to 24 months after closure for legal/tax obligations. Usage analytics are aggregated and anonymized after 13 months.

8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (CCPA in California; PIPEDA in Canada; etc.) you may have rights to:

  • Access a copy of your data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and associated personal data.
  • Object to certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable).

Email privacy@stockanalysis.invalid and we will respond within 30 days. Account deletion will also be available self-serve from settings.

9. Children

The service is not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Security

We use TLS in transit, encrypted storage at rest, hashed (never plaintext) passwords and tokens, and least-privilege access. No system is perfectly secure — please use a unique password and contact us if you suspect compromise.

11. Changes

We will notify you of material changes by email or in-app notice.
